Bio
Music for the space between feeling everything and saying nothing.
GLASHER is an emerging electronic music producer operating at the intersection of Trap and Future Bass — and known to get "housey." With releases through Dim Mak Records (Steve Aoki's storied label), a Beatport charting single, and multiple guest mix spots at Dim Mak Studios on iHeart Radio, GLASHER is building early-stage momentum with credible industry validation behind him.
GLASHER — phonetic of 'glacier' / an extremely large mass of ice which moves very slowly, often down a mountain valley.
